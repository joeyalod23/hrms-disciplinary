1. Getting Started

1.1 Accessing the System

Open your web browser (Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, or Microsoft Edge recommended) and navigate to the system URL provided by your administrator.

1.2 Logging In

Step 1: On the login page, enter your username and password.
Step 2: Click the Sign In button.
Step 3: Upon successful authentication, you will be redirected to the Dashboard.
Note: If you enter incorrect credentials, an error message will appear. Contact your administrator if you have forgotten your password.

1.3 User Roles

RoleDescription
AdminFull system access including user management, audit logs, and compliance configuration.
HRDAccess to all HR operational modules, compliance management, and audit log viewing.
UserDay-to-day operations: data entry, viewing records, and running reports.

1.4 Navigation

The sidebar on the left provides access to all system modules. Click on a menu item to navigate. Collapsible sections group related modules together:

  • HR Management — Employees, Attendance, Manpower, Cases, Contracts
  • Recruitment — PRF Requests, Applicants, PRF Monitor
  • Site Operations — Field Checking, Bunkhouse, ATD, Incident Reports, NTE, NoV
  • Compliance, Reports, Document Vault, Audit Log — Standalone modules
Tip: On mobile devices, tap the ☰ button to show/hide the sidebar.

1.5 Logging Out

Click Logout at the bottom of the sidebar to end your session. Always log out when using shared computers.

2. Dashboard

The Dashboard is the system's home page, providing a real-time overview of key HR metrics.

2.1 Statistics Cards

The top section displays six clickable cards showing:

  • Total Employees — Click to go to the Employees list
  • Present Today — Click to go to Attendance
  • Open Cases — Click to go to Disciplinary Cases
  • Expiring Contracts — Click to go to Contracts with expiring filter
  • Bunkhouse Beds — Click to go to Bunkhouse management
  • Active ATDs — Click to go to ATD management

2.2 Charts

  • Monthly Case Trends — Line chart showing disciplinary cases over the last 6 months
  • Cases by Status — Doughnut chart showing case status distribution
  • Severity Breakdown — Doughnut chart showing Light/Less Serious/Serious offense proportions

2.3 Widgets

  • Today's Tasks — Pending compliance tasks with priority indicators and due dates
  • Recent Cases — Most recently created disciplinary cases with status badges
  • Upcoming Hearings — Scheduled hearings with dates
  • Top 10 Offenses — Most common offenses with COD codes and counts
  • Repeat Offenders — Employees with multiple cases
  • Expiring Contracts — Contracts expiring within 30 days
  • Recent Activity — Latest audit log entries

3. Employees

3.1 Viewing Employees

Navigate to HR Management → Employees to see the employee list. You can:

  • Search by name, employee ID, department, or position using the search bar
  • Filter by status using the status badges (Active, Inactive, Resigned, Terminated)
  • Navigate pages using the pagination controls at the bottom

3.2 Adding an Employee

Step 1: Click the + Add Employee button.
Step 2: Fill in the employee details. Required fields are marked.
Step 3: Click Save to create the record.

3.3 Editing an Employee

Click the Edit button next to any employee in the list. Modify the desired fields and click Save.

3.4 Viewing Employee Details

Click an employee's name to view their full profile, including linked disciplinary cases, contracts, attendance records, and active ATDs.

4. Attendance

4.1 Recording Daily Attendance

Navigate to HR Management → Attendance.

Step 1: Select the month and year using the filter controls.
Step 2: Click + Record Attendance.
Step 3: Search and select the employee.
Step 4: Enter the date, time-in/out (AM/PM/OT), and select the status.
Step 5: Click Save.
Tip: If a record already exists for the same employee and date, the system will update it instead of creating a duplicate.

4.2 Monthly Summary

Navigate to HR Management → Summary to view per-employee monthly attendance summaries with totals for working days, tardiness, leaves, and absences.

4.3 Exporting Attendance

Click the CSV button to download attendance records for the selected month in CSV format for payroll processing.

5. Incident Reports

The Incident Reports module (F-HRD-023 form) is the starting point for the disciplinary due process.

5.1 Creating an Incident Report

Step 1: Navigate to Site Operations → Incident Reports.
Step 2: Click + New Incident Report.
Step 3: Fill in the alleged violator's details, incident information, location, witnesses, and narrative description.
Step 4: Click Save.

5.2 Using OCR for Scanned Forms

Step 1: Click OCR Upload to upload a scanned F-HRD-023 form image.
Step 2: The system will extract text and pre-fill form fields automatically.
Step 3: Review and correct any extracted data before saving.
Note: OCR accuracy depends on image quality and handwriting legibility. Always verify extracted data.

5.3 Issuing NTE from Incident Report

From the incident report view, click Issue NTE to automatically generate a Notice to Explain with pre-filled incident details.

5.4 Viewing & Printing

Click on an incident report to view details. Use the Print button for a print-friendly version.

6. Notice of Violation (NoV)

6.1 Creating an NoV

Step 1: Navigate to Site Operations → NoV.
Step 2: Click + New NoV.
Step 3: Select the linked incident report, enter findings and recommended actions.
Step 4: Click Save.

6.2 NoV Workflow

The NoV follows a four-stage workflow:

  1. Draft — Initial creation, editable by site staff
  2. Submitted to Main Office — Sent for review with date tracking
  3. Reviewed — Main Office provides feedback and notes
  4. Returned to Site — Final status after main office processing

Use the action buttons in the view page to advance the status.

7. Notice to Explain (NTE)

7.1 Creating an NTE

Step 1: Navigate to Site Operations → NTE.
Step 2: Click + New NTE.
Step 3: Select the employee via the search field, choose the offense category (COD-coded), and fill in incident details.
Step 4: Enter the NTE body text — this can be auto-populated if created from an Incident Report.
Step 5: Click Save.

7.2 NTE Lifecycle

  1. Draft — Newly created, editable
  2. Issue/Serve — Record service details: date received, employee signature, or refusal with witness. The 5-day explanation deadline is auto-calculated.
  3. Reply — Record the employee's written explanation
  4. Resolve — Enter the final decision/resolution
  5. Close — Final closure of the NTE

7.3 Preventive Suspension

During the NTE process, you can record preventive suspension details (number of days, effective date, return date) if applicable.

7.4 Printing an NTE

Click the Print button on the NTE view page for a print-friendly version suitable for physical serving.

8. Disciplinary Cases

8.1 Creating a Case

Step 1: Navigate to HR Management → Disciplinary Cases.
Step 2: Click + New Case.
Step 3: Search and select the employee. Assign an offense category (COD-coded).
Step 4: Fill in case details: incident date, description, linked incident report (if applicable), and memo number.
Step 5: Click Save. The system auto-generates a case number (DC-YYYY-NNNN).

8.2 Managing Case Documents

From the case view page, you can:

  • Upload documents — Incident reports, NTEs, investigation reports, verdicts, appeals
  • Delete documents — Remove incorrectly uploaded files

8.3 Adding Case Notes

Internal notes can be added to cases for documentation purposes. Notes are timestamped and show the author.

8.4 Managing Verdict

When a case is resolved, record the verdict details: penalty, decided by, served by, received by, and received date. Upload the verdict document if available.

8.5 Closing a Case

Set the case status to Resolved or Dismissed once all proceedings are complete. Record the closure date and resolution details.

9. Hearings

9.1 Scheduling a Hearing

Step 1: Navigate to HR Management → Hearings.
Step 2: Click + New Hearing.
Step 3: Select the disciplinary case, set the hearing date and time, add notes.
Step 4: Click Save. The parent case status auto-updates to "For Hearing".

9.2 Hearing Status Workflow

Update the hearing status as it progresses: Scheduled → Ongoing → Completed → Cancelled.

10. Contracts

10.1 Creating a Contract

Step 1: Navigate to HR Management → Contracts.
Step 2: Click + New Contract.
Step 3: Search and select the employee, enter contract type, rates, start/end dates, and project site.
Step 4: Click Save. The employee's daily/monthly rates and date_hired are auto-updated.

10.2 Extending a Contract

From the contract view page, click Extend. Enter the extension date range, reason, and memo number. The system auto-updates the contract's end date.

10.3 Viewing Expiring Contracts

Use the Expiring in 30 Days filter to see contracts nearing expiration. These are also highlighted on the Dashboard.

10.4 Exporting Contracts

Click CSV to download all contracts in CSV format.

11. Recruitment & PRF

11.1 Creating a PRF Request

Step 1: Navigate to Recruitment → PRF Requests.
Step 2: Click + New Request.
Step 3: Fill in department, position, trade, skilled/non-skilled counts, and signatories.
Step 4: Click Save.

11.2 Managing Applicants

From the recruitment view page, manage applicants through the pipeline:

  1. For Screening — Initial application received
  2. For Interview — Scheduled for interview
  3. For Medical — Undergoing medical examination
  4. For Orientation — Cleared for onboarding
  5. Hired — System auto-creates employee record
  6. Rejected / No Response — Discontinued

11.3 Pre-Employment Checklist

Track document completeness for each applicant using the pre-employment checklist. Mark items as completed as requirements are submitted.

12. PRF Monitor

The PRF Monitor provides a real-time dashboard of hiring status across all projects.

12.1 Dashboard View

Upon navigating to Recruitment → PRF Monitor, you'll see a summary of all projects with requested vs. hired counts.

12.2 Project Drill-Down

Click on a project to see individual PRF line items with their applicants and hiring status.

12.3 Managing PRFs & Applicants

Within a project view, you can:

  • Create new PRFs — Add new manpower requests
  • Add applicants — Link applicants to specific PRF line items
  • Edit/Delete — Modify or remove PRFs and applicants

Click on any PRF line to view its detailed page with full applicant list and stats.

13. Manpower

13.1 Manpower Loading

Navigate to HR Management → Manpower. The top section shows manpower loading targets by trade. Add new loading entries or delete existing ones.

13.2 Team Management

View all teams with their leadman/foreman assignments and member counts. Click on a team to view its roster.

13.3 Employee Assignments

Assign employees to project sites and teams. The system automatically ends any previous active assignment for the same employee to prevent double-booking.

14. Field Checking

14.1 Creating an Inspection Report

Step 1: Navigate to Site Operations → Field Checking.
Step 2: Click + New Inspection.
Step 3: Enter inspection date/time, project site, inspector, foreman, and leadman details.
Step 4: Add violations found: employee name, designation, violation description, and action taken. Upload photo evidence.
Step 5: Click Save.

14.2 Updating Inspection Status

From the inspection view, update the status: Open → Addressed → Closed as corrective actions are completed.

15. Bunkhouse Management

15.1 Managing Rooms

Step 1: Navigate to Site Operations → Bunkhouse.
Step 2: Click + Add Room to add a new bunkhouse room (room number, capacity, bunkhouse name).

15.2 Managing Occupants

Step 1: Click + Add Occupant to assign someone to a room.
Step 2: Select or search for the employee, or type a name manually for non-employees.
Step 3: Set the start date, rental rate (default Php 100), and frequency (Weekly/Monthly/One Time).

15.3 Checking Out Occupants

Click Check Out on an occupant's record to set the end date and deactivate the assignment. The room's occupant count is automatically updated.

16. Authorization to Deduct (ATD)

16.1 Creating an ATD

Step 1: Navigate to Site Operations → ATD.
Step 2: Click + New ATD.
Step 3: Search and select the employee. Set the weekly deduction amount and date range.
Step 4: Add deduction items (item name and amount). The total deduction is auto-calculated.
Step 5: Click Save.

16.2 Managing ATD Status

Update the ATD status as needed: Active → Completed → Cancelled.

16.3 Exporting ATDs

Click the CSV button to download ATD records.

17. Compliance Calendar

17.1 Viewing Tasks

Navigate to Compliance to see all compliance tasks. Use the filter buttons (All, Pending, Overdue, Completed) to narrow down the list.

17.2 Creating a Task

Step 1: Click + New Task (Admin/HRD only).
Step 2: Enter the title, description, select the module, set the due date and priority.
Step 3: Click Save.

17.3 Auto-Generated Tasks

Click Auto-Generate to create compliance tasks for expiring contracts and pending evaluations automatically. Tasks are color-coded by priority: Urgent (red), High (orange), Normal (blue).

17.4 Completing Tasks

Click the checkmark button next to any task to mark it as completed, or click Mark Overdue to update overdue tasks.

18. Document Vault

18.1 Uploading Documents

Step 1: Navigate to Document Vault.
Step 2: Click + Upload Document.
Step 3: Enter the title, select the document type (Policy, Form, Report, Contract, Other), choose a category, and optionally set a revision number and description.
Step 4: Select the file and click Upload.

18.2 Searching & Filtering

Use the search bar and document type filter to find specific documents quickly.

18.3 Deleting Documents

Use the delete button to remove documents. This permanently deletes the file from the server.

19. Reports

19.1 Viewing Reports

Navigate to Reports to see summary statistics and charts including:

  • Cases by severity and status
  • Department-wise case distribution
  • Monthly case trends
  • Top offenses and their frequencies
  • Average case resolution time

19.2 Exporting Data

Use the export buttons to download CSV files:

  • Disciplinary Cases — All case records
  • Employees — All employee records
  • Attendance — Attendance records
  • Contracts — All contract records
  • Full Export — Combined data from all modules

20. Audit Log

The Audit Log captures every significant action performed in the system. Navigate to Audit Log to view the log.

20.1 Filtering Logs

  • Module filter — Select a specific module to view its entries
  • Search — Search across all log fields

20.2 Clearing Old Logs

Click the Clear button to remove log entries older than 90 days (Admin/HRD only).

21. Troubleshooting

21.1 Cannot Log In

  • Verify you are using the correct username and password
  • Check that Caps Lock is not enabled
  • Contact your administrator to reset your password

21.2 Page Not Loading

  • Check your internet/network connection
  • Ensure the server is running (contact IT support)
  • Try clearing your browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser (Chrome or Edge recommended)

21.3 Data Not Saving

  • Check that all required fields are filled in
  • Verify file sizes are within limits (10MB for documents, 30MB for vault)
  • Check the server's storage space

21.4 OCR Not Working

  • Ensure the uploaded image is clear and legible
  • Handwritten text may have lower accuracy than printed text
  • Large files may take longer to process

21.5 Contact Support

For unresolved issues, contact the system administrator or IT support team.
